Data Analytics is the process of collecting, cleaning, transforming, and analyzing data to extract meaningful insights that support business decision-making. It helps organizations identify trends, measure performance, solve problems, and improve efficiency. Data analysts use tools such as SQL, Excel, Python, Power BI, and Tableau to work with data and create reports and dashboards. For example, a company can analyze sales data to identify its best-selling products, understand customer behavior, and make data-driven decisions to increase revenue. By converting raw data into actionable insights, data analytics plays a crucial role in helping businesses achieve their goals and gain a competitive advantage.
